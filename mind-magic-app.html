Purpose

First working prototype of the Mind Magic app experience. Built to demonstrate core product philosophy and user journey before full development.

✨
Design North Star

Never Leave

Stickiness and identity-level belonging are the north star — not utility features. The app should feel like a community, not a tool.

🗓️
Timeline

April 2026 Target

App MVP to be showcased to broader team. Desired goal is April Course cohort export to use the app as first beta test.

Product Philosophy
"Rewire your mind. Create your life."

The app is designed around identity-level belonging — not transactional engagement. Members don't just use Mind Magic, they become it. Personalised MM agent, context-smart push notifications, vision board uploads, gamification and community citizenship are the building blocks of a product people never want to leave.
